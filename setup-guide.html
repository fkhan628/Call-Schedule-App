📋 DSG Call Schedule

Quick setup to get notifications on your phone and computer — takes less than 2 minutes

Open the Call Schedule App
1

Open the app

Click the button above or go to:

fkhan628.github.io/Call-Schedule-App/

Works on any phone, tablet, or computer browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox).

2

Allow push notifications

When you first open the app, your browser will ask if you want to allow notifications. Click "Allow".

If you don't see the prompt, go to the Setup tab → scroll to the bottom → click ⚙️ Settings → click "Enable Push Notifications".

This lets you receive alerts on your device when the schedule changes, shifts are swapped, or vacation requests need your attention — even when the browser is closed.

3

Select your name

Go to the "My Schedule" tab and pick your name from the dropdown. This tells the app who you are so it can:

  • Show only your shifts and vacations
  • Let you download your personal calendar (.ics)
  • Tag your device for targeted notifications
Your choice is saved on this device — you only need to do this once.
4

Add to your home screen (optional)

For quick access, add the app to your phone's home screen:

iPhone/iPad: Tap the share button (box with arrow) → "Add to Home Screen"

Android: Tap the three-dot menu → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App"

Desktop: Bookmark the page or pin the tab

You're all set!

You'll now receive push notifications when:

  • A new schedule is published
  • Shifts are swapped
  • Vacation requests are submitted or approved
  • Trade requests need your response

Common Questions

I accidentally blocked notifications — how do I fix it?

Chrome: Click the lock icon in the address bar → Site settings → Notifications → Allow
Safari: Settings → Notifications → find the site → Allow
iPhone: Settings → Safari → Notifications → find the site → Allow

Do I need to keep the app open?

No. Once you've allowed notifications, they'll arrive even when the browser is closed. The service worker runs in the background.

Can I get notifications on multiple devices?

Yes. Open the app on each device (phone + computer), allow notifications on each, and select your name on each. You'll get alerts on all of them.

How do I propose a shift trade?

Go to the Requests tab → "Propose a Shift Trade" section. All trades are two-way — you take one of their shifts in return. The other surgeon will be notified and can accept or decline.

How do I request vacation?

Go to the Requests tab → "Request Vacation" section. Fill in your name, dates, and reason. The scheduler will be notified and can approve or deny.

How do I download my personal calendar?

Go to My Schedule tab → select your name → click "Download My Calendar (.ics)". Import the file into Google Calendar, Apple Calendar, or Outlook.

Who do I contact for issues?

Reach out to the schedule administrator (whoever sent you this link).
